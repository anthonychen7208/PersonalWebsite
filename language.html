Japanese

Watching anime in Japanese and reading the subtitles has gotten me to start learning the language. Here's some things I've learned.

There are three writing systems in Japanese. Hiragana, Katakana, and Kanji.

Hiragna and Katakana are phonetic alphabets. Hiragna is round and Katakana consists of straight lines.

Kanji are Chinese characters that represents both sounds and meanings. They were actually introduced into the Japanese language through Korea.

The use of honorary titles is really prevalent in anime, but not used as much in the real world.

San: wide usage. Added for things like a person's characteristics.

Sama: the respectful version of san. Used in the business context.

Kun: refers to a young man below you in social hierarchy.

Chan: refers to children or girls.
