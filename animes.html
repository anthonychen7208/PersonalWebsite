Some Animes I've Watched

Here are some of the anime I've watched!

I've watched a lot of anime this year and each has taught me something important. Here are some lessons I've learned from some of my favorites which I have applied to my everyday life.

Anime Title Length What I Learned
Darker than Black 42 episodes The price of power, treating people different than you right, thinking for yourself, etc.
Your Lie in April 22 episodes Being true to yourself, living life to the fullest, there is beauty in life if you look for it, etc.
Kaguya Sama Love is War 24 episodes The power of communication, learning to rely on others, putting pride aside. etc.
Haikyuu!! 88 episodes Adapting to conflict, we all have our strengths and weaknesses, learning to work with others, etc.
Darling in the Franxx 24 episodes The importance of teamwork, learning to accept others including their flaws, accepting rejection, etc.
Tokyo Ghoul 48 episodes Learning to keep on living, learning to ask for help, seeing new perspectives, etc.
